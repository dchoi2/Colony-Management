Import from Excel

Upload an existing colony spreadsheet (.xlsx) and the app will read the mice from it. You'll see a preview first and can choose which sheets to bring in — nothing is saved until you confirm.

It recognizes the usual columns automatically, in any order: cage, number, sex, dob, dod, geno, tags, breeder pair, parent pair, generation, use, link, and notes. Tabs that aren't colonies (e.g. behavior or locomotor data) are skipped.

Tip: each colony tab becomes its own colony in the app. If a result ever looks off, you can simply open that colony and delete it to redo.
